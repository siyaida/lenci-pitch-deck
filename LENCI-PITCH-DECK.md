## [SLIDE 2] — THE PROBLEM

### The Photoshoot Is Broken

Traditional product and fashion photography is **slow, expensive, and impossible to scale**.

| Pain Point | Reality |
|---|---|
| **Cost** | A single fashion photoshoot costs **15,000–60,000 SAR** (studio, photographer, models, styling, post-production) |
| **Time** | **2–6 weeks** from booking to final deliverables |
| **Logistics** | Coordinate 5–10 people, locations, permits, weather, wardrobe |
| **Iteration** | Want a different background? **Re-shoot everything.** New model? **Start from scratch.** |
| **Scalability** | 200 SKUs × 5 angles × 3 models = **3,000 photos** — months of work |
| **Saudi-Specific** | Limited local studios, models, and creative talent pool. Brands often outsource abroad — losing cultural authenticity |
| **Modest Fashion Gap** | Thoob, abaya, and modest fashion brands struggle to find studios that understand their aesthetic and cultural requirements |

> **E-commerce brands spend 10–30% of their product launch budget on photography alone.**
> Most of that budget is wasted on logistics, not creativity.
